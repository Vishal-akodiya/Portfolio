Architectural Summary

Inspired by the viral moments that moved the internet, this is a cozy, heartfelt journey about three unlikely friends finding their way Home: The Nihilist Penguin: Walking toward a peak he was told he’d never reach. Wilson the Cock: Finding courage after being rescued from the river. Punch the Monkey: A lonely soul trading his heartache for a new family.

Core Deliverables

  • • Combination of 2D and 3D
  • • Adaptive Sound Effects
  • • Shader Compatibility of 2D in 3D
  • • Optimized Draw-Call Mesh Combining

System Breakdown

This was one of my favorite projects because it taught me a lot. The most challenging part was making a 2D shader react to 3D lights, which I solved by writing my own custom shader. I also implemented an Adaptive Sound System so the player hears audio that changes dynamically according to the environment
